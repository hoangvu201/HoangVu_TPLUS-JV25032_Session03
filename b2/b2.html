Sơn Tùng MTP

Sơn Tùng M-TP is a Vietnamese singer-songwriter, actor, and model, known for his contributions to the music industry. He was born in Thái Bình province and currently resides in Ho Chi Minh City. He gained fame after participating in the fourth season of Vietnam Idol and debuted with the song “Cơn Mưa Ngang Qua”. He is also the founder of M-TP Entertainment, which manages his music career and productions. For more about his music, you can visit his official YouTube channel.
